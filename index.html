Our AI Technology

EfficientNet Transfer Learning

We use EfficientNet, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network architecture that uniformly scales the depth, width, and resolution of the network for improved performance.

Our model was fine-tuned on the Food101 dataset, containing 101 food categories with 1,000 images per category.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the nutrition analysis?

Our model achieves over 85% accuracy on food identification. Nutrition values are calculated based on standard portions.

Can it identify home-cooked meals?

Yes, our AI can identify most home-cooked meals, though complex dishes with multiple components might be analyzed based on the most prominent ingredients.

Is my data private?

We prioritize your privacy. All uploaded images are processed securely and not stored permanently without your permission.
